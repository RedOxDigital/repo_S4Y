How do I get started with Socials 4U?

Simply schedule a free 30-minute consultation call. We'll discuss your goals, current social presence, and determine if we're a good fit. If we are, you'll receive a custom proposal within 48 hours, and we can start onboarding within a week.

What information do you need from me initially?

We'll need access to your social accounts, any existing brand guidelines, your logo and brand colors, insight into your target audience, business goals, and examples of content you love. We provide a comprehensive onboarding questionnaire to make this easy.

Do I need to have existing social accounts?

Not at all! We can help set up new accounts from scratch or optimize existing ones. If you're starting fresh, we'll handle everything from account creation to profile optimization, ensuring you launch with a strong foundation.

What industries do you work with?

We work with diverse industries including e-commerce, healthcare, wellness, beauty, professional services, real estate, hospitality, and more. Our team has experience across B2C and B2B sectors, adapting our strategies to each industry's unique needs and audience.

What platforms do you manage?

We manage Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Twitter, and YouTube. We recommend focusing on 2-3 platforms where your target audience is most active, rather than spreading thin across all channels.

Do you provide content creation services?

Yes! We offer professional photography, videography, graphic design, copywriting, and editing. We can create all content in-house or work with assets you provide. Our team includes skilled content creators who produce scroll-stopping visuals.

Do you handle community management?

Absolutely. We monitor and respond to comments, DMs, and mentions daily. We engage with your community authentically in your brand voice, answer questions, and foster meaningful conversations that build loyalty and trust.

Do you run paid advertising campaigns?

Yes! We offer social media advertising services including campaign strategy, ad creative development, targeting, budget management, and optimization. We can manage Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and Pinterest ads with proven ROI.

How much do your services cost?

Packages start at $997/month for basic management (1 platform), $1,497/month for standard (2 platforms with content creation), and $2,997/month for premium (3+ platforms, full-service). Custom enterprise solutions available. All packages include strategy, posting, and reporting.

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

No hidden fees! The monthly rate covers everything: strategy, content creation, posting, community management, and reporting. For new clients, there's a one-time onboarding fee of $500 to cover initial strategy development and account optimization.

What are your contract terms?

We offer flexible month-to-month contracts with no long-term commitments. However, we recommend a minimum 3-month partnership to see meaningful results. You can cancel anytime with 30 days' written notice. No penalties, no hassle.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We accept monthly payments via credit card, ACH, or wire transfer. For annual commitments, we offer a 10% discount when paid upfront. We use secure payment processing through Stripe for your protection and convenience.

What's the typical onboarding timeline?

Onboarding takes 5-7 business days. Week 1 includes discovery calls, brand audit, and strategy development. Week 2 covers content calendar creation, approval process setup, and account optimization. We start posting high-quality content by day 10.

How is content approved before posting?

We use shared tools like Asana, Monday, or Google Drive for content approval. You'll receive your monthly content calendar by the 25th of each month for the next month. You can review, request revisions, and approve via comments. We accommodate rush approvals when needed.

How do you measure and report on performance?

We provide detailed monthly reports covering key metrics: engagement rate, follower growth, reach, impressions, website clicks, and conversions. Reports include insights, recommendations, and strategy adjustments. We also offer quarterly strategy review calls.

How often will we communicate?

You'll have direct access to your account manager via email, Slack, or phone. We schedule monthly strategy calls, send weekly progress updates, and are available during business hours for quick questions. For urgent matters, we respond within 2 hours.

What if I'm not happy with the service?

We're committed to your satisfaction. If you're unhappy, we'll work immediately to address concerns with revised strategy, new content, or process changes. With month-to-month terms, you can cancel anytime. Most issues are resolved through open communication.

What happens in case of a crisis or negative comments?

We have crisis management protocols in place. For negative comments, we respond professionally and empathetically according to best practices. For serious issues, we'll alert you immediately and collaborate on the response. We never delete comments unless they're spam or violate policies.

Can I pause my service temporarily?

Yes! We understand business needs change. You can pause service for up to 60 days per year without penalty. Just provide 14 days' notice. During the pause, you won't be charged, and we can resume when you're ready without re-onboarding.

Do you provide training for my team?

Absolutely! We can provide social media training sessions for your team covering best practices, content creation tips, platform updates, and analytics interpretation. Training sessions can be one-time or ongoing, customized to your needs.
